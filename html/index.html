ClassMate Idea Hub

A practical platform for College of Computing students at the University of Jeddah to organize their team project ideas by course and major.

One place where teams can submit proposals and instructors can quickly see who is working on what.

Submit Your Team Idea Browse All Projects

For Students

Register your group, link your project to a specific course, and clearly describe your idea, tools, and major.

For Instructors

View all submitted ideas in one page, filter by course or major, and understand the scope of each team project.

For Teams

Keep a clean record of who is in your group, who is the representative, and what the project is about.
